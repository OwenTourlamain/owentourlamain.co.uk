MIG Joins Network

We're delighted to welcome Mr Independent Games to The Whodok Network.

Mr Independent Games is a start-up indie games platform based in Milwaukee. They will be working under the guidance and with the assistance of Whodok's vast team of specialists.

Michael, of Whodok, said: "Mr Independent Games is the first platform to join The Whodok Network and this marks a turning point for our company. We hope they benefit from being a part of our rapidly expanding network!"
