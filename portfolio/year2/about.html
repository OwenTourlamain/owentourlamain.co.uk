Design

Before starting my assignment, I conducted some research on the many online shops that exist on the Internet. I focused on music shops shop as iTunes, Amazon and Google Play, for ideas that would make my music shop as easy to use and as attractive as theirs. For general functionality of my website, I took inspiration from not only different types of online retailers but also from other general websites. By doing this, I discovered that by using Google Play and various Microsoft pages as inspiration, I would be able to achieve a website, that would not only be functional but looks good.

Before conducting this assignment, I have had previous experience in PHP, as I have made server-side scripted websites in the past. As a result of this, I had a decent understanding of how HTML and PHP were related and therefore,was pretty confident before making my website. Other modules last year enabled me to strengthen my knowledge of HTML and CSS and therefore was already comfortable with these. Therefore when starting the website I had a clear vision of what my music shop was going to look like and felt comfortable in my ability to be able to do this

Evaluation

I think that my website does not meet most of the specified requirements, and as such, probably deserves a low mark. However I think that the parts of my site that do work, work very well, I think my site looks good and is very user friendly. I think my use of the Itunes API to get the album art is the simplest and most effective way to flesh out the site. Although that feature would have benifited from server-side caching to improve page load times.
